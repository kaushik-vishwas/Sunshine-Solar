RACK MOUNT BATTERY

About product

The LFP Battery Rack Mount is a member of the ES SERIES from SUNSHINE and is a high-performance lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO4) battery. This battery offers a number of features that make it ideal for a wide range of applications including UPS systems, inverters, and solar energy storage.

One of the key features of this battery is its smart indicator which allows for easy monitoring of the battery’s charge level and overall health. This feature helps users to easily determine when the battery needs to be recharged, and can help prolong the life of the battery by preventing over-discharge. The LFP Battery Rack Mount also boasts a high energy density, which means that it can store a large amount of energy in a compact and lightweight package. This makes it ideal for applications where space is limited, and for use in portable or mobile systems. In addition, this battery is designed with high efficiency cells which provide a high level of performance and minimize energy loss. This results in increased battery life and faster charging times. Another important feature of this battery is its eco-friendly design. The battery is made with non-toxic materials, and is free from hazardous substances such as lead and cadmium. This makes it a safe and responsible choice for both the environment and for users. The LFP Battery Rack Mount also requires no maintenance, which means that users can simply install it and forget about it. This eliminates the need for regular checking and maintenance, which can be both time-consuming and costly. The battery also comes with a number of protections such as Overload, Short Circuit, Battery Deep Discharge, Battery Over Charge, and Over temperature. These protections ensure that the battery is protected against damage and prolongs its lifespan. Finally, the LFP Battery Rack Mount is fully compatible for solar charging, which makes it an ideal choice for use in solar energy storage systems. This allows users to harness the power of the sun to charge the battery, and to enjoy the benefits of clean and renewable energy. In summary, the LFP Battery Rack Mount is a high-performance lithium iron phosphate battery that offers a number of features that make it ideal for a wide range of applications. With its smart indicator, high energy density, high efficiency cells, space-saving design, low weight, eco-friendly design, zero maintenance, high battery life, fast charging, and compatibility for solar charging, it is a versatile and reliable choice for users.
